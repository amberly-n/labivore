Our Mission

Labivore: an animal or person that eats food of lab origin.

We are quickly becoming labivore's more than we realize. We might even have been labivores earlier than we think.

We aim to demystify lab grown meat, to build a culture around meatless meat, and unlock new cuisines previously unimaginable. Our safe space allows people from all cultures to enjoy the food of previous generations without worrying about the environmental and ethical consequences of their food choices. Our pristine, lab to table experience prevents viruses from passing on from animals to humans.


Our Story

The onset of Covid-19 brought another onset of “pandemic xenophobia scapegoating,” similar to the ones the world has seen before like with Ebola and the Spanish Flu. The Labivore’s Dilemma was created in the heart of NYC’s Chinatown, one of the areas that was most impacted during the pandemic. Our scientists and chefs have developed the menu to tackle the collapse of human integrity and racial aggressions around food.

Our founders traveled around the world exploring wet markets and Eastern cuisines to find out for themselves whether the Western rhetoric on the East is as uncivilized, impoverished, unsanitary, and crowded as people say it is. They found the media was wrong, and there are many more nuances to why things are the way they are now.

By developing the formulas and recipes to allow for more sustainable and humane practices of meat eating, our community has welcomed us with open arms and we have been able to educate our customers about good food practices.
